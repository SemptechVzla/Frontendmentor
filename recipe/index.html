Simple Omelette Recipe

And easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredientes

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablepoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs
